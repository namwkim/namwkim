NAM WOOK KIM

I am a Ph.D. student in the computer science department at Harvard. I work in the Visual Computing group led by Hanspeter Pfister. My resesarch investigates ways to make complex data more approachable to the publc by designing communicative visualization tools and building data-driven applications. My graduate studies are partially supported by Kwanjeong Educational Foundation.

Prior to Harvard, I was a research associate at KAIST and also worked in industry for 4 years, including Samsung Techwin (now part of the Hanhwa group) and LG Electronics. I received M.S. in Computer Science from Stanford Univ. and B.S. in Computer Science, Digital Media, and Mathematics from Ajou Univ. and Stony Brook Univ. (dual degree).
